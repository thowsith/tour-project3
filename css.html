Welcome to Dream Travel

Madras Travels and Tours (MTT) is a leading travel management company based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 2004, the company is ISO certified (ISO 9001: 2015) and approved by the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India. We are Franchise partner Lufthansa City Center, Chennai. We are a IATA (International Air Transport Association) accredited agent. We are a preferred travel agent for MNC's, manufacturing companies, hoteliers and leading educational institutions, Madras Travels provides high-quality travel services to the market globally. Our comprehensive range of services including Domestic and International Holiday Packages, Air-Ticketing, Hotel Bookings, MICE Arrangements Visa and Passport Assistance, Car rental and Luxury coaches Madras Travels aims to deliver exceptional travel experiences to its client
